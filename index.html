Welcome to Challa's website

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am working with Dr. Amy Marshall-Colon and Prof. Matt Hudson on systems biology of bioenergy (Sorghum, Miscanthus, and Sugarcane) and food (soybean, corn, wheat) crops. I received my Ph.D degree from South Dakota State University with Dr. Wanlong Li on wheat and soybean genomics. My Ph. D. dissertation work focuses on the use of high through-put or the second generation sequencing technologies like 454/Roche (which is obsolete now) and Illumina (Hiseq, Miseq, Nextera etc), to analyze genomes and transcriptome to understand molecular mechanisms, metabolic pathways and gene networks involved in the manifestation of agronomically important traits like root growth and development, wax synthesis, trichome development etc.
